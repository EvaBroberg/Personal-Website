ME OUTSIDE WORK

For the most part, my spare time isn't that much different from the working days. I enjoy learning and working on personal projects. My main points of interests consist of drawing, designing, and coding. I used to illustrate a lot, but lately, I spend a lot more time attending coding events and meetups such as codebar, watching tutorials on udemy and trying to build exciting little projects. I do stop sometimes though! And when I do... below are the activities closest to my heart.

I have a slight obsession with small dog breeds (especially chihuahuas). On the left, you can see my furry babies Chibi and Bella and you can (obviously) follow them on instagram. These two are my ultimate stress relief. They also make sure I peel my eyes off the computer screen from time to time and go outside. They also make sure I socialize with people outside of work as I attend chihuahua meetings and doggie-socials!

I was born and raised in Lithuania, I live and work in London, but my heart belongs to Finland. That's my happy place. It's where I go when life becomes too much. So I am learning Finnish language. This has been going on for almost a year and it definitely has been a journey! But I'm starting to feel pretty confident, so if you can speak Finnish to me!

Hiking and nature is life! Whenever I need a proper break I try to go to nature as much as possible. As they say "I would walk 500 miles And I would walk 500 more". My perfect holiday is in the cottage in the middle of nowhere, preferably with sauna and lake view, no people and trees as far as you can see!

I am a massive human rights supporter and a feminist. I think there is nothing more important than equality, people should feel free to love and to be whoever they want wherever they want, as well as to be valued and appreciated in the workplace the same regardless of gender.
